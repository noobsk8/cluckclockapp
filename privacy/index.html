CluckClock Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

1. Overview

CluckClock values your privacy. This policy explains what data we process and why.

2. Data Stored on Device

CluckClock stores core app data on your device, including:

  • alarm settings
  • chicken/progress state
  • sound and appearance preferences
  • onboarding/settings state

3. Notifications and Alarm Permissions

CluckClock requests notification/alarm permissions to deliver core functionality. You can change permissions anytime in iOS Settings.

4. iCloud Sync (If Enabled in Future)

If iCloud sync is added/enabled, app data may sync through your Apple ID services. No separate CluckClock account is required for Apple-managed sync.

5. Analytics and Purchases (If Enabled in Future)

If analytics, purchases, or virtual currency are introduced, this policy will be updated to describe what is collected, why it is collected, and how long it is retained.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Any service providers used (if introduced) are limited to operating the app.

7. Data Rights and Deletion Requests

For privacy questions or deletion requests, contact privacy@cluckclock.app or support@cluckclock.app.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always appear on this page with an updated date.
