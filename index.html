CluckClock Heavy sleepers and habitual snoozers who need more friction. Mini-games, weekday/weekend split, virtual pet accountability, sharing. Less minimal than a basic alarm app because the wake-up flow is intentionally more involved.
Default iPhone alarm People who already wake up reliably and want simplicity. Fast, familiar, built into iPhone. Very easy to snooze or dismiss half-awake if standard alarms already are not working for you.
Challenge-based alarm apps People who want tasks or challenges before dismissing an alarm. Mission, puzzle, or challenge-style alarm dismissal. Can feel more utility-first and less memorable if you want accountability and personality, not just a jolt awake.
Basic loud alarm apps People who mainly want louder sound options without extra game layers. More volume and sound variety, usually with simpler dismissal. Louder sound alone may not help much if your real problem is repeated snoozing.
