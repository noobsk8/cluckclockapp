Why You Struggle to Wake Up

It’s not laziness. The science explains why standard alarms fail—and why CluckClock is built for the way your brain actually works.

Sleep inertia

The grogginess you feel when the alarm goes off is a real transitional state. Your brain is still shifting from sleep to wake—and cognitive performance is temporarily lower. Hitting snooze or solving a simple math problem doesn’t require enough engagement to truly clear that fog.

ADHD & time blindness

If you have ADHD, “time blindness” and executive dysfunction make it easy to ignore or dismiss alarms. Standard alarms don’t create enough emotional stakes or immediate, tangible consequences—so your brain doesn’t prioritize getting up.

The Worm Mini-Game That Actually Wakes You

A short, focused mission that forces your eyes and hands to engage—without burning you out before you’ve had coffee.

4–6 second reaction mission

Tap the three worm plots in a 2×2 grid. Unlike competitors that demand math or squats, this is a tiny pet-care game that clears your head in seconds. Enough to break sleep inertia; not so much that you give up.

Fake Worm traps

Tap a fake worm and you lose. The trap mechanic forces real visual focus and coordination—so you have to be truly awake before the sound stops. No half-asleep tap-and-dismiss.

Permanent Stakes & The Graveyard

When your chicken dies, it stays dead. That’s the point.

  • Permanent death — Dead chickens don’t come back. You start a new chicken with a new personality and name.
  • The Graveyard — A visual record of every chicken you’ve lost (and how long they lived). A feature no other alarm app offers: your wake-up history, honest and permanent.

Consistency Rewards: The 3-Day Egg

Stay consistent; get rewarded. A healthy chicken lays an egg every 3 days.

Play-to-earn mornings. Feed your chicken three worms, day after day, and it stays healthy. Every 3 days it lays an egg—unlocking progress toward new chicken tiers (Monthly Sentinel, Yearly Phoenix) and giving you a tangible reward for showing up.
