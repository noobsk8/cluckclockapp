CluckClock vs Alarmy

Both apps are built for people who ignore standard alarms, but they solve the problem differently. CluckClock leans into mini-games, accountability, and a persistent chicken system. Alarmy is better known for challenge-based dismissal tasks.

Download CluckClock

Who each app is for

CluckClock is strongest for people who want a wake-up app with personality, gentle accountability, and a reason to take care of a virtual pet instead of mindlessly snoozing. Alarmy can fit people who want challenge-based dismissal without the chicken layer.

Where CluckClock stands out

  • Alarm dismissal through worm and shake mini-games
  • Separate weekday and weekend chickens
  • Virtual pet accountability tied to chicken health
  • Permanent Graveyard history when a chicken dies
  • Shareable live and dead chicken cards

Choose CluckClock if...

  • You need more than a one-tap dismiss flow and want a wake-up app that feels more involving.
  • You like the idea of mini-games plus a virtual pet that gives your mornings accountability instead of generic alarm challenges.
  • You want separate weekday and weekend alarm identities rather than one flat alarm list.
  • You want something more memorable and playful, including shareable live and dead chicken screens.

Choose Alarmy if...

  • You want a more utility-first challenge alarm without the chicken, Graveyard, or pet-style consequence loop.
  • You care most about challenge-based dismissal and do not want the wake-up experience wrapped in a game-like theme.
  • You prefer a more traditional alarm-app feel over CluckClock’s stronger personality and stakes.
